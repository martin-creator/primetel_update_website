For Funders

Invest in proven, data-driven healthcare that reaches Tanzania's most underserved.

  • $20 provides care for a family of five for an entire month
  • $3,000 expands mobile outreach to a new community for six months
  • $18,000 scales our USSD platform to reach 5,000+ users
  • $50,000 upgrades an entire rural health corridor

Transparent reporting. Real impact. Every dollar tracked and celebrated.

For Partners

We co-create programs with schools, clinics, nonprofits, tech companies, and universities. If you care about youth, mental health, maternal care, or tech-for-health—let's talk.

Potential collaborations:

  • Research partnerships
  • Digital health innovation
  • In-kind donations (devices, medications, airtime)
  • Curriculum co-development
  • Joint outreach programs

Join Our Fellowship

Are you a Tanzanian changemaker under 35? The Primetel Public Health Fellowship selects 10 emerging leaders each year for a hands-on experience in health innovation, outreach, and advocacy.

Fellows receive:

  • Monthly stipend
  • Professional mentorship
  • National exposure
  • A chance to lead real programs
Apply to the Fellowship
